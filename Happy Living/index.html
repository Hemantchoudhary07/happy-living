What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is a normal human emotion that we all experience at some point in our lives. It can be helpful in certain situations as it prepares us for danger or helps us to focus on important tasks.

However, when anxiety becomes excessive and begins to interfere with daily life, it may be a sign of an anxiety disorder

Anxiety is a global mental health issue that affects people of all ages and backgrounds. Anxiety is a common mental health problem in India, affecting people of all ages and backgrounds.

How is Anxiety Affecting You?

If you are facing anxiety, you cannot perform at your capabilities as it affects the performance of your mind and body

1. Affects Career: Anxiety affects your productivity, creativity, logical thinking, and confidence.

2. Affects Health: Facing anxiety continuously for a long time means you are under continuous stress. This increases the chances of Hypertension and other psychosomatic diseases.

3. Affects Relationships: Anxiety does not only affect you as a person but affects the relationships you share because you are always focused on yourself and you are not present to have a conversation with your loved ones.
